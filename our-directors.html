Kayode Aderibigbe holds a bachelor’s degree in Banking and Finance from Olabisi Onabanjo University. He is an experienced Banker with over 20 years of experience in Banking and Accounting. He is currently the Head of Internal Audit & Compliance at KC Microfinance Bank Limited, a position he has held since December 2022. Before joining the services of the Bank, he worked as the Branch Operations Manager (UBA), Internal Control officer (Equatorial Trust Bank)-now Sterling Bank; and Head of Operations/Credit (Trade Bank Plc). He was formerly a Managing Director/CEO of Igangan Microfinance Bank Limited in Oyo state. Kayode Adreibigbe also worked at Meridian Microfinance Bank Limited, Lagos as Head of Internal Audit/Compliance from where He joined KC Microfinance Bank Limited. He has attended several local and international courses including Banking Operations, Risk Management, Credit Administration, Internal Audit /Control, etc. He is an Associate member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria (CIBN), Institute of Bankers of Nigeria (ICAN) and Microfinance Certified.
